I’m YB, a UI/UX designer based in Providence.


Currently, I am studying Computer Science at Brown University. Brown is where I discovered my love for design and actively gave back to the design community by creating & teaching a class on human-centered design, as well as hosting workshops on design tools. I employ the finest UX practices to provide clear, uncomplicated, and user-friendly designs for web and mobile applications. Considering corporate objectives and technology limitations, I work to make the interaction between users and the interactive products they use simpler in every project.
